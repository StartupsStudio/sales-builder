# From Lead to Close in Hours: How AI Accelerates the Sales Cycle

Traditional B2B sales cycles: 30-90 days. AI-powered sales cycles: 4-48 hours. The difference isn't magicâ€”it's removing every moment of friction.

## The Old Sales Cycle

**Traditional B2B SaaS (45-day average):**

- Day 0: Lead comes in
- Day 1-3: SDR qualifies (if they respond)
- Day 7: First demo scheduled
- Day 14: Demo happens (if prospect shows)
- Day 21: Proposal sent
- Day 28-35: Back and forth on terms
- Day 42-45: Contract signed

**Where time is wasted:**
- Waiting for SDR to respond (hours/days)
- Scheduling conflicts (days/weeks)
- Waiting for proposals (days)
- Internal approvals (days/weeks)
- Contract red lines (weeks)
