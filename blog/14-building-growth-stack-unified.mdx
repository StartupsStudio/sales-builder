# Building Your Growth Stack: Content + Social + Outbound + Funnels Unified

The traditional growth stack: 15 disconnected tools, 8 logins, 3 dashboards, zero coordination. The 2026 growth stack: One platform, AI-orchestrated, unified execution.

## The Old Way: Tool Chaos

**Typical SaaS Growth Stack (2024):**

1. **Content:** WordPress + Copywriter freelancers
2. **SEO:** Ahrefs + Semrush
3. **Email:** SendGrid + Customer.io
4. **Outbound:** Apollo + Outreach
5. **Social:** Buffer + Hootsuite
6. **CRM:** HubSpot or Salesforce
7. **Analytics:** Google Analytics + Mixpanel
8. **Video:** Vimeo + hired videographer
9. **Forms:** Typeform
10. **Calendar:** Calendly
11. **Support:** Intercom
12. **Payment:** Stripe
13. **Docs:** DocuSign
14. **Chat:** Slack (internal coordination)
15. **Project Management:** Notion/Asana

**Cost:** $3K-$10K/month
**Coordination:** Manual, fragile, time-consuming
**Visibility:** Fragmented across dashboards
**Problem:** Nothing talks to each other
