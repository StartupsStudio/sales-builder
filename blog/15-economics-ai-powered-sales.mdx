# The Economics of AI-Powered Sales: 50x Output at 10% of the Cost

The math is brutal: AI agents deliver 50x more output at 10% of the cost. Traditional sales teams can't compete economically. Here's the breakdown.

**10-Person Sales Team Annual Cost:**

**Salaries & Compensation:**
- 4 SDRs @ $70K base + $30K commission = $400K

- Email (SendGrid): $3.6K/year

- **Subtotal: $94,800**

**Training & Development:**
- Onboarding: $40K
- Ongoing training: $20K
- Conferences: $30K
- **Subtotal: $90,000**

**Management Overhead:**
- Recruiting: $50K

**TOTAL ANNUAL COST: $1,945,800**
