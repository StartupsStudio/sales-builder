# Analytics for Autonomous Growth: What to Measure When AI Runs Your Funnel

Traditional metrics: Opens, clicks, conversions.
AI-era metrics: Agent effectiveness, channel orchestration, omnichannel attribution.

Here's what to measure when AI runs your growth.

## The Shift in Metrics

**Traditional Analytics (Human-Driven):**
- Individual channel performance
- Last-touch attribution
- Campaign ROI
- Human productivity (emails sent/day)

**Autonomous Analytics (AI-Driven):**
- Cross-channel orchestration
- Multi-touch attribution
- Agent effectiveness
- System intelligence (AI learning rate)

## What NOT to Measure

**Vanity Metrics (Don't Obsess):**
- Total followers (unless converting)
- Total page views (unless converting)
- Email list size (unless engaged)
- Posts published (unless driving results)

**Focus on:**
- Followers who engage
- Page views that convert
- Email subscribers who buy
- Posts that drive revenue

## Weekly Review Checklist

```typescript
const weeklyReview = {
  // 30-minute weekly review
  checks: [
    {
      metric: 'MRR growth',
      question: 'Are we growing 10-15% MoM?',
      ifNo: 'Analyze: CAC up? Conversion down? Churn up?'
    },

    {
      metric: 'AI agent performance',
      question: 'Are all agents meeting targets?',
      ifNo: 'Which agent underperforming? Why? Fix or replace?'
    },

    {
      metric: 'Channel performance',
      question: 'Which channels over/underperforming?',
      ifNo: 'Shift budget from underperforming to overperforming'
    },

    {
      metric: 'Funnel bottlenecks',
      question: 'Where are prospects dropping off?',
      ifNo: 'Optimize that stage—biggest impact on revenue'
    },

    {
      metric: 'Unit economics',
      question: 'Is CAC staying low? LTV:CAC ratio healthy?',
      ifNo: 'Problem with acquisition cost or retention—dig in'
    }
  ],

  actions: [
    'Apply AI recommendations (1-click)',
    'Adjust budgets based on ROI',
    'Fix identified bottlenecks',
    'Double down on what's working'
  ]
}
```

## The Bottom Line

In 2026, analytics for AI-powered growth focus on:

1. **Business outcomes** (MRR, customers, profit) over vanity metrics
2. **AI agent effectiveness** (quality, learning, autonomy)
3. **Cross-channel orchestration** (multi-touch attribution, synergies)
4. **Economic efficiency** (CAC, LTV:CAC, channel ROI)
5. **Continuous optimization** (AI recommendations, bottleneck fixes)

The result: Clear visibility into what's working, what's not, and what to do about it.

---

**Action Steps:**
1. Set up comprehensive analytics dashboard
2. Define targets for key metrics
3. Review weekly (30 min) and monthly (2 hours)
4. Apply AI optimization recommendations
5. Double down on what's working, cut what's not

The age of gut-feel growth is over. The age of data-driven, AI-optimized growth is here.
